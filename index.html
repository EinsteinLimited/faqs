FAQs

What is WeLinQ?

WeLinQ is an app which features card collection to understand our relations by playing question card game online and offline - a unique way to make relationships deeper.

Who can make collection on app?

Any user can make their own card collections. This way user can share their questions which they think helps in longer term in relationship.

How do I play virtual card game?

Simply open the app. Choose a collection you want to play. Select the contact you want to send the virtual card.

Once card question is shared, both can answer the question. Answers are revealed only when both persons have written their answers.

Is WeLinQ free?

Yes! The app is completely free and will remain so.

What is community in WeLinQ?

WeLinQ has an active community to answer questions from their better understanding of subject.

Purpose of community is to help each other and answer some specific relationship related questions.
